💊 Pharma Trail — Payment Dose-Response

Avg Medicare Part D claims per prescriber, by drug-specific industry payment (program year 2024 · public CMS data · correlation, not causation).

More payment → more prescribing, for the branded drugs. Metformin (control) has only the $0 bar — it gets no payments. Humira stays flat. The $1k–10k / $10k+ bars are tiny samples (Eliquis $10k+ = 3 prescribers) — noise, not signal.
